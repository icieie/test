KEP1ER: 2022 RISING STAR


Kep1er, a girl group formed through a survival show called Girls Planet 999, has finally made a successful debut on the 4th of January 2022, having 'First Impact' as their first mini-album with 'WA DA DA' as the title track. Read more...

With their successful debut, they have been breaking records and smashing the charts with tops and highest tallies. Their first album has already broken an all-time record of highest-selling album copies. Their mini-album First Impact sold more than 152,000 copies in South Korean stores, which surpasses aespa's 147,000. Kep1er has now set the record for the most sales of a South Korean girl band debut album. Their first MV also had above 10 million views in 24 hours. The rookie group's promotions have been going well with performances appearing on Mnet, Music Bank in KBS WORLD TV, Studio Choom, and more to go. Link to official article here.
